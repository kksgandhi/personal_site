Biopic games: Games that illustrate the experience of someone unlike you.

 - And Roger (1 hour): See through someone's elses eyes. An incredible game, but unfortunately very prone to spoilers. Highly recommend, but it's also very emotionally intense.
 - Before your eyes (1.5 hours): The story of someone's life. What makes a life full and valuable? Saying any more would spoiler it.
 - The Beginner's Guide (1.5 hours): The life of a game designer, wondering why we make art.
 - Florence (1.5 hours): The challenges of being in a young-love relationship
 - Last day of June (3 hours): An old man reminisces about his life
 - Dujanah: Living with grief in a chaotic world.
 - Consume me (7 hours): Living with an eating disorder
 - Unpacking (4 hours): See the life of someone just by unpacking their things as they transition through life.
 - To a T
