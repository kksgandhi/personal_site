transformational games

This note last modified March 14, 2022

Games designed to transform the player by making them learn something, see things in a different way, feel some emotion, etc.

Transformational games are an umbrella term for serious games, educational games, Games with a purpose, etc.

philosophical games do not require authorial intent, but transformational games do, and thus transformational games are a subset of philosophical games.
