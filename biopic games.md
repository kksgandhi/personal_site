Games that illustrate the experience of living as someone

 - The Beginner's Guide (1.5 hours): The life of a game designer, wondering why we make art.
 - And Roger (1 hour): Life as someone who perceives things very differently than "normal". Hard to describe without spoiling it. Warning, very emotionally intense.
 - Before your eyes (1.5 hours): The story of someone's life. What makes a life full and valuable?
 - Florence (1.5 hours): The challenges of being in a young-love relationship
 - Last day of June (3 hours): An old man reminisces about his life
 - Consume me (7 hours): Living with an eating disorder.- 
 - Unpacking (3.5 hours): The story of someone's life, told through the things they unpack after a move.
