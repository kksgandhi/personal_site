Problems like the housing crisis, inequality, hell, even bullshit like traffic, are not problems where we need more intelligence. We *know* the solutions to these problems, but can't implement them because of politics.

In the video above, Hank argues that more intelligence won't fix our problems; wisdom will, the wisdom of knowing what problems are worth solving, how to apply our intelligence to problems.
