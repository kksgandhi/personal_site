    My interests primarily lie in educational games, with a specific focus on games that promote critical thinking and philosophy. Why read about the trolley problem when you can play through it yourself? These interests arose from a course I taught in undergraduate where I used games like The Stanley Parable, The Talos Principle, and The Witness to teach philosophy
